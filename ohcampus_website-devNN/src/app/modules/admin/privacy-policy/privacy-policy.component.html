We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes :

b. Information We Collect Automatically :
  • Device Information : IP address, browser type, operating system, and mobile device details.
  • Usage Data : Pages viewed, search queries, click-throughs, and time spent on the platform.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies : Data collected through cookies and similar tools to enhance user experience.
c. Information from Third Parties :

We use your information to:

  1. Provide personalized course, college, and admission recommendations.

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  1. Access and Update : Review and update your personal information through your account settings.

5. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and safeguard your account.

OhCampus.com is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us to have it removed.
